About Us

Why Arctic Plumbing and Heating? At Arctic Plumbing and Heating, we know living in Alaska means harsh and unpredictable weather conditions. Heating your home, freeze ups, and plumbing issues are a key component to living in the last frontier and we are committed to ensuring our experienced technicians complete each job with quality and care from beginning to end the first time so you can rest assured that your home or business will be warm, and ready to brave the cold.
